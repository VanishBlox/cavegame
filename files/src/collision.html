collision.aabb (ax, ay, aw, ah, bx, by, bw, bh)
Axis-Aligned Bounding-Box overlap check.

Parameters:

  • ax: x of box A
  • ay: y of box A
  • aw: 1/2 width of box A (distance from the edge of the box to the center)
  • ah: 1/2 height of box A (distance from the edge of the box to the center)
  • bx: x of box B
  • by: y of box B
  • bw: 1/2 width of box B (distance from the edge of the box to the center)
  • bh: 1/2 height of box B (distance from the edge of the box to the center)

Return value:

    boolean
